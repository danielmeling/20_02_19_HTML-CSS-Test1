The Issue of Groundlessness in our Times

1. Groundlessness

There are many issue in our times that make apparent that we need to learn to live without foundations. Accordingly, the concept of sunyata can help to let go of concepts and beliefs we cling to.

2. Non-Clinging

However, never make a belief out of sunyata! This would be like turning the medicine into poison! This is an important insight, maybe! Is it?

3. Not Clinging to Groundlessness

Sunyata is not a thing. It is also nothing. It is simply a way to say: It's a dumb question to ask for an ultimate ground! Let go!
